Privacy Policy

At VyapaarPay Finance, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our loan advisory services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as name, phone number, and email address
  • Loan-related information including loan type, amount, and purpose
  • Communication records from our interactions
  • Technical data like IP address and browser information for website analytics

How We Use Information

Your information is used solely for providing loan advisory services:

  • To assess your loan eligibility and provide personalized guidance
  • To connect you with appropriate banks or NBFCs
  • To communicate about your loan inquiry and provide support
  • To improve our services and website functionality

Data Protection & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure data storage and encrypted transmission.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with banks, NBFCs, or other financial institutions solely for the purpose of facilitating your loan application. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies like Google Analytics and Meta Pixel to analyze website usage and improve user experience. These tools help us understand how our site is used and optimize our services.

User Consent

By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. You have the right to withdraw consent or request deletion of your data at any time.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at support@vyapaarpayfinance.com.
